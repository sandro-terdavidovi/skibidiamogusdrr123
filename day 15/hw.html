Favorite Georgian Dishes

Khachapuri

Cheese-filled bread, popular in Georgia.

Many varieties like Adjaruli and Imeruli.

Dough filled with cheese and baked.

Mix dough, add cheese, bake 15-20 min at 220°C.

Khinkali

Traditional Georgian dumplings filled with meat and spices.

Boiled and served hot, often eaten by hand.

Filling typically includes minced beef or pork with herbs.

Prepare dough, fill with meat mixture, shape dumplings, boil until done.
